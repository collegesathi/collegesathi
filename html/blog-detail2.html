Top 10 Online/Distance Education/Correspondence BA (Bachelor of Arts) Colleges/Universities In India

by John Philips - April 13, 2023

Top 10 Distance BA Colleges in India

As we will discuss in this blog that there are hundreds of top colleges available in the country that are offering the course of distance BA in their curriculum. Distance BA is a very popular course all over the country and it has a multi-disciplinary nature. In this blog, we are going to describe the Top 10 Distance education universities offering B.A. in India. You can have a look at the details of these colleges as well as the eligibility of the B.A. course.

You can do BA after passing any stream and can also choose any subject or specialization in the BA distance course. When it comes to choosing the top distance universities in India, students usually get confused for making their decision.

Types of Bachelor of Arts (BA) Courses Available

But, we are making this clear for you as you can find out the top 10 distance BA colleges of India given below:

  1. IGNOU Distance - The most popular and oldest open universities you can find in India is Indira Gandhi National Open University. As IGNOU was founded in 1985, it was India's first national Open University.

    You can choose from the hundreds of courses in the list of courses offered by IGNOU at UG and PG level. Students get all the advantages in terms of flexibility in the length of the courses after obtaining admission to the IGNOU. The fees charged for Ignou BA distance course is 6 K - 8.1 K.

  2. Symbiosis Distance University - Symbiosis Distance Learning College (SCDL) was established in 2001. Symbiosis has been working for various years in the country and this is the reason that it has become one of the largest private universities operating for many years in the field of distance education.

    Symbiosis College of Distance Learning (SCDL) has been approved by AICTE. And, the distance education courses are granted recognition by UGC-DEB. The fee for the distance BA course is 26,000.

  3. Sikkim Manipal Distance University - The BBA course at Sikkim Manipal University is a great choice for students who want a degree in management from one of the countrys top colleges. For the selection of courses, distance education at Sikkim Manipal University is highly popular as the distance learning institute and the fee for the courses is quite affordable as compared to other regular programs. The distance course of BA in Sikkim Manipal University has been approved by UGC-DEB. The fee for the distance BA course is 26,000.

  4. Chandigarh Distance University This universitys main campus is located in Punjab. Chandigarh University has been accredited with an A+ grade from NAAC and it has also been approved by UGC-DEB.

    The option of choosing a distance program from CU will surely help the students as you can get all the advantages regarding the duration and fees. The fee for the distance BA course is 22,000.

  5. Annamalai University - Annamalai University Distance Education is one of the oldest universities providing distance education to students for many years. As per UGCs latest notice, Annamalai University was providing open & distance learning programs to students without any further UGC-DEB approval since 2015.   6. School of Distance Learning, Delhi University- The School of Open Learning was established as a constituent educational body of the University of Delhi in the year 1962. In India, SOL is one of the greatest names in the field of distance education in India. It is one of the largest educational institutes in India affiliated by University of Delhi. The fees charged for BA distance course form Delhi Universitys is 3,530.

  7. Suresh Gyan Vihar Distance University - Suresh Gyan Vihar University is one of Indias leading private-owned universities that offers BA distance education courses and various other management programs for UG and PG. The SGV University has been certified by NAAC for grade A and UGC-DEB has validated the distance BBA course. The fee of distance BA course from SGVU is Rs. 21,000.

  8. Netaji Subhas Open University - The name of Netaji Subhas Open University is definitely included in the list of the Top 10 universities offering B.com distance programs.

    The Netaji Subhas Open University campus is located in Kolkata, in the Indian state of West Bengal. In the year 1997, it was created. It is also UGC accredited and affiliated with Netaji Subhas Open University. The fees of distance BA course is Rs. 6,300.

  9. Tamil Nadu Open University - Tamil Nadu Open University is one of the best distance education universities situated in the state of Tamil Nadu, in Chennai. It was established in 2002 and it has been affiliated with the Open University of Tamil Nadu.

    Tamil Nadu Open University offers 96 courses in 13 categories, namely Commerce and Banking, Arts, Management, Scientific, IT, Vocational, Law, and many more. The fee of Distance BA charged is Rs. 6,600

  10. University of Madras- At present, the Institute of Distance Education offers 97 degree programs: in South India, the Institute of Distance Education UNON IDE of Madras University is very famous for its various UG & PG programs in distance mode and teaching methodologies.

    Madras University provides English, Historical Studies, Naturopathy and Yogic Sciences through Distance Education. Distance BA fees in the University of Madras is Rs. 14,570.

Distance Education For Bachelor Of Arts In India

In India, the course of Bachelors of Arts commonly abbreviated as BA is one of the oldest degrees that were firstly introduced in the country. India is the worlds only nation with the largest number of young people, which means that about half of Indias population is less than 25 years of age. With so many young people falling into the college students group, it is also very difficult to fit them all in standard schools.

Therefore, in such a situation, the importance of distance learning and education in the correspondence mode becomes significant. There are several universities that offer this online BA course to the students to help them in providing classes and sessions online and save their time as there is no need to go physically to the colleges. The BA or Bachelors degree in Arts course is a 3-year undergraduate degree chosen by students who want to graduate from a specific specialization or subject. It is one of the students top choices after completing their 12th class. The best part of the online BA course is that it is a multi-disciplinary program, and students can opt for their bachelors degree from all streams.

Eligibility Criteria of Distance BA Course In India

  • Students are required to pass class 12th examination from any stream either Science/Commerce/Arts from a recognized board to become eligible for an online BA.
  • All the working professionals who cannot attend physical classes can also pursue the Online BA program.
  • Candidates who want to do graduation in a particular subject can also do the online BA in that subject.
